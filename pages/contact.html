The best way to experience Serenity Collective is to join us for one of our community gatherings. All are welcome, regardless of where you are in your journey.

What to Expect

  • A warm, welcoming environment
  • No judgment, only grace and support
  • Opportunities to share or simply listen
  • Prayer, worship, and community

Taking the First Step

We understand that reaching out can feel overwhelming. Please know that there's no pressure, no judgment, and no expectations beyond showing up as you are.

"Jesus meets us exactly where we are, not where we think we should be."

Whether you're struggling with addiction, relationships, trauma, or simply feeling lost, we're here to walk alongside you. Your journey toward healing and freedom can begin today.
