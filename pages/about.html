About Serenity Collective

We are a community of people who love Jesus deeply and believe with all our hearts in the power of complete and total restoration. We've each walked our own path—some smooth, many broken—and we've come to know this one truth: Jesus meets us exactly where we are, not where we think we should be.

Out of that truth, we created a 12-step, Jesus-centered guide born from real life and real transformation. It's not just a process—it's a journey of healing, honesty, surrender, and hope. No matter your past, your pain, or the weight you carry, this path is for you.

We believe addiction wears many faces. It's not just the obvious chains—it's anything that keeps us from freedom, from joy, from the fullness of life God has for us. Anything you go to for comfort, warmth, or security outside of Jesus, we consider to be an addiction or dependency. This includes not only substances, but also behaviors, relationships, work, food, shopping, entertainment, or any pattern that becomes our source of comfort instead of Christ. And freedom? It's not a distant dream. It's a promise. One that Jesus invites us into, step by step, day by day.

We don't claim to have it all together. We're just people who have tasted grace and can't help but share it. Our prayer is that this guide becomes a lifeline for those who feel lost, a mirror for those ready to see themselves clearly, and a door for those longing to walk into the light.

You're not alone. There is hope. There is healing. And in Jesus, there is always a way forward.
