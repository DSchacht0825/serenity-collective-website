Serenity Collective Pilot Program Release of Liability

Thank you for your interest in participating in our Jesus-centered 12-step recovery pilot program. Before joining, please read and acknowledge the following release of liability.

Release and Waiver of Liability

I, the undersigned participant, acknowledge and agree to the following terms regarding my participation in the Serenity Collective pilot program ("Program"):

1. Program Nature and Purpose

I understand that the Serenity Collective pilot program is a faith-based, Jesus-centered 12-step recovery program designed for spiritual growth, community support, and personal transformation. This program is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or psychiatric treatment.

2. Voluntary Participation

I acknowledge that my participation in this program is entirely voluntary. I may discontinue my participation at any time without penalty or consequence.

3. Release of Liability

In consideration for being permitted to participate in the Program, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue Serenity Collective, its organizers, facilitators, volunteers, and any affiliated organizations from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, or causes of action whatsoever arising out of or relating to any loss, damage, or injury that may be sustained by me while participating in the Program or any activities related thereto.

4. Assumption of Risk

I understand that participation in group discussions, spiritual activities, and personal sharing involves certain inherent risks. I voluntarily assume all risks associated with my participation in the Program, whether or not such risks are known, anticipated, or foreseeable.

5. Medical and Professional Care

I acknowledge that the Program facilitators are not licensed medical professionals, therapists, or counselors unless otherwise specified. I understand that nothing learned or shared in the Program constitutes professional medical, psychological, or legal advice. I am encouraged to seek appropriate professional help if needed.

6. Confidentiality and Sharing

I understand that group discussions may involve personal sharing and that confidentiality is maintained within the group. I agree to respect the confidentiality of other participants and understand that my participation may involve sharing personal information.

7. No Guarantee of Results

I understand that Serenity Collective makes no guarantees regarding the outcomes or effectiveness of the Program for any individual participant. Results may vary, and success depends on many factors including personal commitment and individual circumstances.

8. Emergency Situations

I understand that in case of emergency situations or concerns about safety, program facilitators may need to contact appropriate authorities or emergency services.

Acknowledgment

I have read and fully understand this Release of Liability. I acknowledge that I am signing this agreement freely and voluntarily, and intend my signature to be a complete and unconditional release of all liability to the greatest extent allowed by law.

By proceeding with the pilot program registration, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to the terms outlined in this Release of Liability.
