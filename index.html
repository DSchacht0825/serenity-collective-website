Our Path to Restoration

We believe healing happens through four interconnected pillars that create a foundation for lasting transformation in Jesus.

1

Community

Building meaningful connections and support networks where we can be authentic about our struggles and victories.

2

Coaching

Personal guidance and mentorship for your journey, walking alongside others who understand the path.

3

Worship

Connecting with God through praise and prayer, encountering His presence and transformative love.

4

Discipleship

Growing in faith and following Jesus, developing spiritual maturity and practical wisdom for life.

These four elements work together to create a holistic journey of healing and growth.

Our Heart

"We don't claim to have it all together. We're just people who have tasted grace and can't help but share it."

We believe addiction takes many forms beyond substance abuse - including behavioral addictions, codependency, and any life-controlling issues that keep us from God's freedom. We offer a 12-step, Jesus-centered guide to transformation that addresses the whole person: mind, body, and spirit.

What is Faith-Based Recovery?

Faith-based recovery combines evidence-based addiction treatment with Christian spiritual principles, providing community support, biblical counseling, worship, prayer, and discipleship alongside traditional recovery methods.
